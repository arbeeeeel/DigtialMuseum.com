WELCOME TO DIGITAL MUSEUM

The Archives and Museum of Olivarez College houses the historical records of the school. These are primary source documents that have accumulated over the years. Likewise, this section cares for a collection of artifacts and other objects of scientific, artistic, or historical importance to the Olivarez family and makes them available for public viewing.
